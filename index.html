About Us


Born as a gift box business during lockdown 2020 as a side hustle while home
schooling, and to this day still owned and run by one Mum!
From a tiny online biz run from a kitchen table, to a little Pop-Up shop 8
months later when lockdown restrictions finally eased, to an amazing candy
store on the Main Street of my home town, all within 12 months!
Sugar Sugar still has so much room for growth, with the ultimate goal being to
build the business into a franchise and to open more stores all over Australia!
Just a girl with big dreams, and a passion for creating something amazing.
It’s not just any old candy store, it’s a space filled with childhood memories
and 90’s nostalgia. It is essentially my inner child in the form of a very black,
yet very bright and colourful retro candy shop 💖
